Xiao Shi

Ph.D. Student in Generative AI in the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, UW–Madison

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Generative AI at Computational Imaging Group(CIG), advised by Prof. Ulugbek S. Kamilov.

My research focuses on generative modeling, inverse problems, distribution shift, and medical image analysis. I am broadly interested in developing reliable and data-efficient algorithms that leverage modern generative models to solve imaging problems under real-world noise and measurement constraints.

Before joining UW-Madison, I earned my B.S. in economics from Central University of Finance and Economics, M.S. degree in Data Analytics and Statistics from Washington University in St. Louis, and M.S. degree in Management from Carnegie Mellon University.
